Your passport application number

CK752704 P

To complete your passport application, follow the steps below
Step 1

Use white A4 paper. Portrait orientation. Black ink only.

Print it right
We accept single or double-sided printing. Make sure nothing's cut off at the top or bottom of each page. In your printer settings, choose the option closest in meaning to 'Fit to page/printable area'. If you're using US legal paper, choose the option closest in meaning to 'none/not required'. We also accept US letter size.
Step 2
Gather original documents

See item 3 on the checklist. We don't accept certified copies.
Step 3
Get passport photos

Make sure they comply with our requirements
Step 4
Go to Australia Post

Find a participating Australia Post outlet to lodge your application checklist.

What's an application checklist?

Your checklist is a short form of your application that you lodge at Australia Post. Item 3 on the checklist sets out all the documents you need to bring with you.

You have 28 days to lodge your checklist before it expires. After that, you'll have to create a new one.

You only have until 25 June 2024 to lodge your checklist

If you don't lodge it by then, you’ll have to create a new one.

Download your record of responses

What’s a record of responses?

Your record of responses contains every answer you gave us to the questions in this online application.

You can access your record of responses from your AusPassport account for the next 45 days. After that, we'll delete it. So save a copy for your records now!
